Ocean Cargo: Specialized International Auto Shipping & Logistics

Your Trusted Partner for Global Vehicle Transport
With over 15 years of industry experience, Ocean Cargo is a specialized provider of international vehicle shipping and logistics services. We are built on personalized support, uncompromising quality, and a commitment to efficiency, professionalism, and client satisfaction.

Auction Account Management:

Our multilingual team (English, Spanish, Arabic, Russian, Urdu, Pashto) provides expert assistance in opening and managing accounts with Copart, IAAI, and Manheim auctions.

Export Documentation Expertise:

Receive precise guidance on all vehicle export paperwork, streamlining compliance and ensuring seamless customs clearance.

Secure U.S. Operations:

• Baltimore, Maryland • Savannah, Georgia • Houston, Texas • New York, New York • Los Angeles, California
