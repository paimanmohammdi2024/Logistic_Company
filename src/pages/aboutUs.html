About Us

Since our company was established, our mission has been: Provide individual approach to every customer Focus on quality, not quantity Value the time of each client by handling all freight arrangements on their behalf Set competitive prices for our services, our experienced team will assist you to open an account with Auctions Site (Copart, IAAI) and maintain. We will also advise you regarding vehicle export documentation understanding. With Ocean cargo you get a diverse team of professionals with more than 15 years of experience in the logistics industry Ability to speak and communicate fluently in various languages including English, Spanish, Arabic, Russian, Urdu and Pashto. Four Loading warehouses in Houston–TX, Savannah–GA, New York–NY and Los Angeles–CA Ocean cargo ships your vehicle to over 100 countries all over the world. We will deliver your shipments seamlessly and with the utmost care and attention. We will store your vehicles in our warehouse for up to 30 days for free. We will Ship your vehicle to your destination port without worrying about storage.
